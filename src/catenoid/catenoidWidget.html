Catenoid

Parametric representation

$$ \mathbf{x}(u,v) = \bigl( a\,\cosh(\tfrac{v}{a})\cos u,\; a\,\cosh(\tfrac{v}{a})\sin u,\; v \bigr), \quad u \in [0,2\pi), \; v \in \mathbb{R}. $$

Rotational / implicit form

$$ r(z) \;=\; a\,\cosh\!\Bigl(\tfrac{z}{a}\Bigr), \qquad x^2 + y^2 \;=\; a^2\,\cosh^2\!\Bigl(\tfrac{z}{a}\Bigr). $$

Property

The catenoid is a minimal surface, i.e. the mean curvature vanishes everywhere: \( H \equiv 0 \).
